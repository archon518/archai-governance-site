ARCHAI Stack

A five‑layer governance architecture for agentic AI

ARCHAI decomposes agentic AI governance into five tightly integrated layers, each responsible for a distinct dimension of control and accountability.

Identity Substrate

IDENTARCH™

Establishes cryptographically anchored identity for agents, systems, and organizational actors, forming the substrate for trust, attribution, and access control.

Intent Binding

INTENTUM™

Binds organizational intent to agent behavior, creating a traceable, enforceable record of what AI systems are authorized to do and why.

Memory Governance

MNEMARCH™

Governs how AI systems retain, recall, and apply organizational knowledge, with full auditability and control over memory access, scope, and persistence.

Agent Orchestration

AGENTUM™

Defines authority, delegation chains, and operational boundaries for agentic systems, ensuring agents act within governed organizational constraints.

Algorithmic Accountability

ACCOUNTUM™

Ensures every consequential AI output has a traceable authority chain and documented rationale, enabling post‑hoc review, challenge, and redress.
