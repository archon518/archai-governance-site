The ARCHAI Stack

A five‑layer governance architecture for agentic AI.

IDENTARCH™ — Identity Substrate

Establishes cryptographically anchored identity for agents, systems, and organizational actors.

INTENTUM™ — Intent Binding

Binds organizational intent to agent behavior with a traceable, enforceable authorization record.

MNEMARCH™ — Memory Governance

Governs how AI systems retain, recall, and apply organizational knowledge with full auditability.

AGENTUM™ — Agent Orchestration

Defines authority, delegation chains, and operational boundaries for agentic systems.

ACCOUNTUM™ — Algorithmic Accountability

Ensures every consequential AI output has a traceable authority chain and documented rationale.
