ARCHAI, Intentium, and related systems are currently described based on design intent, not completed empirical validation.

All claims about what these systems "do" should be read as:
- what they are designed to do,
- what they are intended to support,
- not as guarantees of current, proven behavior.

Verification status for all systems: pending.
No system in this discipline should be treated as empirically validated until explicit verification work is done and recorded.
