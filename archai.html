Agent Orchestration

AGENTUM™
Agentic Governance

AGENTUM™ governs how agents operate, how authority flows through them, and how they coordinate. It applies its rules — delegation limits, escalation paths, and operational boundaries — where agentic behavior intersects organizational governance. AGENTUM™ ensures agents act within structured authority, not freeform autonomy.

Intent Binding

INTENTUM™
Intent Architecture

INTENTUM™ binds organizational intent to authorized AI behavior. It records intent, validates authority, and links approval to execution whenever an action requires formal authorization. INTENTUM™ ensures AI behavior is anchored to explicit, documented human intent.

Memory Governance

MNEMARCH™
Organizational Memory

MNEMARCH™ governs memory retention, access, and revocation across agentic systems. Its rules apply wherever information is stored, retrieved, or restricted within the governance domain. MNEMARCH™ ensures organizational knowledge is retained appropriately and accessed responsibly.

Algorithm Accountability

ACCOUNTUM™
Accountability Architecture

ACCOUNTUM™ establishes accountability structures for governed AI decisions. It captures rationale, authority lineage, and audit-ready evidence at the points where decisions carry governance weight. ACCOUNTUM™ ensures every consequential output is explainable, attributable, and reviewable.
