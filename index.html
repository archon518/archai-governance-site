What is ARCHAI v2?

ARCHAI v2 is the unified governance architecture for agent systems. It defines how identity, intent, memory, execution, risk, policy, and human oversight interlock into a coherent, auditable structure.

Core Fabric

  • IDENTARCH — Identity Substrate
  • INTENTUM — Intent Binding
  • MNEMARCH — Memory Governance
  • AGENTUM — Execution & Orchestration
  • ACCOUNTUM — Accountability Spine

Control Ring

  • PERIMARCH — Operational Boundary
  • RISKUM — Risk Tiering & Modes
  • TEMPORUM — Temporal Governance
  • POLICUM — Policy Enforcement

Ecosystem Ring

  • SYNERGARCH — Multi-Agent Coordination
  • HUMANUM — Human-in-the-Loop
  • METARCH — Meta-Governance
