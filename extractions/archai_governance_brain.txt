ARCHAI GOVERNANCE BRAIN MODULE

PURPOSE:
Provides meta-governance, policy evolution, oversight, and system-wide ethical alignment. Sits above Policy Layer and Supra-Cortex as the highest governance authority.

COMPONENTS:
- Meta-Governance Engine (MGE)
- Policy Evolution Core (PEC)
- Ethical Alignment Matrix (EAM)
- Governance Oversight Loop (GOL)
- Directive Arbitration System (DAS)

META-GOVERNANCE ENGINE:
- Oversees all policies across the architecture.
- Ensures long-term alignment with system goals.
- Integrates signals from Supra-Cortex and Policy Layer.

POLICY EVOLUTION CORE:
- Evolves policies based on historical data.
- Uses Memory Engine (LTM + IMM) for pattern extraction.
- Generates new policy candidates for review.

ETHICAL ALIGNMENT MATRIX:
- Maintains global ethical constraints.
- Ensures all modules operate within allowed boundaries.
- Blocks directives that violate ethical invariants.

GOVERNANCE OVERSIGHT LOOP:
- Continuously monitors system behavior.
- Detects governance drift or misalignment.
- Issues corrective governance directives.

DIRECTIVE ARBITRATION SYSTEM:
- Resolves conflicts between:
  - Supra-Cortex directives
  - Policy Layer rules
  - Safety Shell constraints
- Prioritizes safety > ethics > coherence > performance.

EVENTS:
- onGovernanceShift()
- onPolicyEvolution()
- onEthicalViolation()
- onDirectiveConflict()
- onOversightAlert()

API:
- governance.update()
- governance.evaluatePolicy(rule)
- governance.issueDirective(target, payload)
- governance.getEthicalState()
- governance.getStatus()

ARCHAI USE:
Provides the highest-level governance, ensuring the entire architecture remains aligned, ethical, stable, and coherent across long operational horizons.
