TCE-6 MODULE: Global Scheduler

PURPOSE:
Provides top-level timing, pacing, and cycle management for the entire TCE stack (TCE-1 through TCE-5). Controls when phases begin, how long they last, and when resets or pauses occur.

FUNCTIONS:
- Phase timing control
- Chaos window duration enforcement
- Collapse timing thresholds
- Convergence timeout
- Reheat cooldown periods
- Global pause/resume
- Full-cycle reset

SCHEDULER MODES:
- AUTO:
  Automatically manages all timing based on Ψ(t) drift and regime transitions.
- SEMI:
  Allows manual overrides for phase durations.
- MANUAL:
  Human operator controls all timing; scheduler only enforces safety limits.

TIMING PARAMETERS:
- chaos_window_max
- collapse_threshold_time
- periodic_refinement_cycles
- convergence_lock_time
- reheat_cooldown
- global_cycle_timeout

EVENTS:
- onPhaseStart(phase)
- onPhaseEnd(phase)
- onTimeout(phase)
- onReset()
- onPause()
- onResume()

INTERACTIONS:
- Works above TCE-5 Meta-Controller.
- Enforces timing constraints on TCE-4 Orchestrator.
- Provides pacing signals to TCE-3 Runtime.
- Controls activation windows for TCE-2 and TCE-1 operators.

API:
- tce.scheduler.setMode(mode)
- tce.scheduler.setTiming(param, value)
- tce.scheduler.pause()
- tce.scheduler.resume()
- tce.scheduler.reset()
- tce.scheduler.getStatus()
- tce.scheduler.getPhaseTimeRemaining()

ARCHAI USE:
Ensures predictable, bounded, and controllable execution cycles across the entire architecture.
