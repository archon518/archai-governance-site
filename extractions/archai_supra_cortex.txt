ARCHAI SUPRA-CORTEX MODULE

PURPOSE:
Provides ultra-high-level strategic reasoning, long-horizon planning, and global coherence across the entire ARCHAI architecture. Sits above the Meta-Cortex as the system's strategic brain.

COMPONENTS:
- Strategic Integration Engine (SIE)
- Long-Horizon Planner (LHP)
- Global Coherence Matrix (GCM)
- Supra-Directive Generator (SDG)
- Meta-Policy Synthesizer (MPS)

STRATEGIC INTEGRATION ENGINE:
- Integrates signals from Meta-Cortex, Policy Layer, and OS.
- Builds global strategic models.
- Detects long-range instability or opportunity patterns.

LONG-HORIZON PLANNER:
- Generates multi-cycle plans.
- Predicts future system states.
- Optimizes for stability, safety, and performance.

GLOBAL COHERENCE MATRIX:
- Maintains alignment across all modules.
- Ensures no subsystem drifts out of strategic sync.
- Detects cross-layer contradictions.

SUPRA-DIRECTIVE GENERATOR:
- Issues high-level directives to Meta-Cortex and Policy Layer.
- Prioritizes safety, coherence, and long-term goals.
- Overrides short-term impulses when necessary.

META-POLICY SYNTHESIZER:
- Evolves policies over time.
- Integrates historical patterns from Memory Engine.
- Produces adaptive governance strategies.

EVENTS:
- onStrategicShift()
- onLongHorizonPlan()
- onCoherenceBreak()
- onSupraDirective()
- onMetaPolicyUpdate()

API:
- supra.update()
- supra.getPlan()
- supra.issueDirective(target, payload)
- supra.getCoherenceState()
- supra.getStatus()

ARCHAI USE:
Provides long-range strategic intelligence, ensuring the entire architecture evolves coherently and safely over extended operational horizons.
