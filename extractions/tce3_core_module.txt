TCE-3 MODULE: Transient Chaos Engine (Core Specification)

PURPOSE:
Implements the full transient-chaos-driven exploration → convergence cycle
based on Ψ(t) drift and bifurcation-aligned behavior.

COMPONENTS:
- Drift Operator (TCE-OP1)
- Chaos Window Operator (TCE-OP2)
- Regime Selector (TCE-OP3)
- Reheat Operator (TCE-OP4)

BEHAVIOR:
1. Initialize Ψ(t) from Φ₀ or prior state.
2. Enter chaotic regime for exploration.
3. Allow monotone drift to move Ψ(t) downward.
4. Pass through inverse period-doubling collapse.
5. Enter periodic regime for refinement.
6. Enter convergent regime for finalization.
7. Optional: invoke reheat operator to reverse drift and re-explore.

ARCHAI USE:
- Autonomous exploration cycles.
- Self-stabilizing decision processes.
- Drift-driven governance transitions.
